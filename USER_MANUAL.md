# 📖 MMS Industries - User Manual

Welcome to the **Manufacturing Management System (MMS)**. This manual provides a step-by-step guide on how to navigate and utilize the features of the application.

---

## 👥 1. For Regular Users

### 🆕 Getting Started
- **Registration:** Create an account via the `Register` page. 
- **Login:** Access your dashboard using your email and password.
- **Profile Management:** Visit the **Accounts Center** (My Profile) to update your name, email, or security settings. This page uses a premium Meta-style sidebar for easy navigation.

### 🛍️ Browsing & Ordering
- **Product Catalog:** Browse high-graphics industrial products on the home page.
- **Filtering:** Use the search bar or category chips to find specific items.
- **Product Details:** Click on any product to see detailed specifications and stock availability.
- **Placing Orders:** Choose your quantity (noting the minimum bulk order requirement) and place your order.

### 🎙️ Using the AI Voice Assistant (MMS-AI)
- **Activation:** Click the floating Chat icon in the bottom right corner.
- **Vocal Modes:** Switch between **Jarvis** (Male/Cinematic) and **Friday** (Female/Professional).
- **Direct Talk:** Toggle "Direct Talk" to speak directly to the AI without typing. The AI will respond with its specialized voice.
- **Bilingual Support:** The AI understands and speaks both **English** and **Hindi**. 
  - *Tip:* The Hindi voices are audio-engineered to simulate cinematic personalities (Pawan Singh/Shreya Ghoshal).
- **Capabilities:** You can ask the AI about products, check your order status, or even ask it to "Place an order for 500 Steel Pipes" autonomously!

---

## 👔 2. For Administrators

### 📈 Admin Dashboard
- **Access:** Admins must log in via the specialized `Admin Login` portal.
- **Inventory Management:** View real-time stock levels. Low-stock items are highlighted in red.
- **Manufacturing Pipeline:** Monitor products through the stages of: *Raw Materials → Processing → QA → Packaging → Shipping*.

### 👥 Employee Management
- **Attendance Tracking:** View attendance statistics for employees.
- **Security:** Administrative tools are protected by role-based access control. The AI will refuse to show sensitive stats to non-admin users.

---

## ❓ 3. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: The AI is speaking too fast.**
*A: The system is tuned to a cinematic rate of 0.9. If it feels off, ensure your browser's default speech settings are at normal speed.*

**Q: How do I change my voice to Hindi?**
*A: Open the Chat window, select 'HI' from the language dropdown, and the AI will automatically switch its brain and voice to Hindi.*

**Q: My order says 'Pending'.**
*A: All new orders start as 'Pending' in the manufacturing pipeline. Once the admin processes them, the status will update in your 'My Orders' section.*
